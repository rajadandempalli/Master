## ✨ Overview
**Petals Paradise Events** is a premium event decor and party rental boutique serving the **DMV (DC, Maryland, Virginia)** area. We specialize in transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary celebrations through curated aesthetics, high-quality rentals, and professional design services.

Whether you're planning a grand wedding, a milestone graduation, an intimate baby shower, or a traditional cultural ceremony, we provide the elegance and reliability your special moments deserve.

## 🏛️ Our Offerings

### 🛋️ Premium Rentals
From the foundation of your seating to the atmosphere of your space:
- **Elegant Seating:** Gold Chivari chairs, adult folding chairs, and hand-carved accent chairs.
- **Tables & Tents:** Round and rectangular folding tables, cocktail tables, and large 16x26 wedding tents.
- **Food Service:** Professional buffet food warmers and serving sets.

### 🎨 Specialized Decor
We bring life to your vision with:
- **Marquee Magic:** 4FT Marquee numbers and "GRAD" marquee letters.
- **Themed Backdrops:** Custom photo backdrops, moon swings, and pipe-and-drape setups.
- **Neon Vibes:** "Happy Birthday," "Congrats Grad," and "Good Vibes Only" neon signs.

### 🏵️ Cultural Traditions
Specializing in exquisite South Asian traditional decor:
- **Haldi & Mehandi:** Traditional Urli, Maiyan Tubs, and Mehandi Umbrella sets.
- **Ceremonial Sets:** Seemantham and Baby Shower backdrop setups.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser.
- A local server (like Live Server or `npm run dev`) to view the interactive features.

### Installation
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/PetalsParadiseEvents/Master.git
   ```
2. Navigate to the project directory and open `index.html`.
